Raised in the Land of the Long White Cloud and currently based in Melbourne, Zoe is a creative sort who dabbles in many areas that fall within the nebulous category of design. From traditional and vector illustration to publication design, packaging, photography and hand lettering, she loves giving everything a go. She is inspired by the quirks of day-to-day life, and is always on the lookout for new ideas. In her spare time she's either contemplating the mindblowing vastness of outer space, or thinking about food.
